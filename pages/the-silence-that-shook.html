The Silence that Shook

There was nothing left to say. Nothing to prove. The body had gone silent. The soul had revealed itself.

In the space where breath makes no sound, I heard it. It was an ancient beat. A forgotten vibration.

It was neither a voice, nor a thought. It was a memory. A memory of who I was before I became me.

I was no longer a man. Nor a name. I was the trembling of silence. The memory of stars. The echo of a truth that needed no explanation.

The world around me faded. Not because it was dying… but because I was being born.

I remained. Present. Empty. Full. Aware.

And in that vibrant void, I knew I had never been separated. That there was no return. Because I had never left.

— Zari-Onn
